ALL ABOUT RAVIE

FOUNDATION

Founded in June 2026, RAVIE was born from a simple observation: Small content creators have incredible talent and potential but rarely get the chance to work with brands. At the same time, brands struggle to find fresh, authentic talent who truly understand content creation and generate real viewership. We saw this gap and decided to bridge it.

Our platform connects these talented creators with brands looking for massive, genuine reach. Creators get the opportunity to grow, showcase their skills, and work directly with brands, while brands get authentic content that spreads awareness to a massive audience. Instead of just guessing what works, we supply our creators with the exact strategic frameworks they need, guiding them to create their best work and drive unified momentum.

FOUNDERS

He takes time connecting with and understanding creators, their style, their goals, and what makes their content stand out. We, Ayush and Anand, are here to build infrastructure for potential talents to connect with brands, generate viewership, and expand their ideas.

Together, we build RAVIE.

VISION

We understand content because we live it. We know what makes an idea work, what makes a brand collaboration feel real.

We want RAVIE to be known as the infrastructure that helps the creator economy grow, the one that understands new-age content and helps both talent and brands get the most out of it. Not by chasing numbers, but by building real, lasting partnerships deeply rooted in good content and genuine potential.
